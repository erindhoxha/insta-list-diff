How use InstaListDiff?

  • Step 1. Download Your Data from Instagram: First, you'll need to download your data from Instagram. Instagram provides an option to download certain information about your account, including your list of followers and followings, in a JSON format. This can be done through your Instagram settings. You can view the steps from Instagram's website. To see all results, in Date range choose "All time".
  • Step 2. Paste JSON Code: You'll see two textboxes labeled "Followers" and "Followings." Simply paste the JSON code containing your list of followers into the "Followers" box, and paste the JSON code containing your list of followings into the "Followings" box.
  • Step 3. Analyze the Results: After pasting the JSON code, click on the "Compare" or "Find Difference" button to analyze the data. InstaListDiff will then process the information and provide you with the differences between your followers and followings.
  • Step 4. Review the Results: You'll receive a clear breakdown of who follows you but you don't follow back, and vice versa. This can be helpful for managing your Instagram connections and understanding your network better.

Frequently asked questions

  • Why is it safe? Client-side Processing: Our app operates entirely on the client side, meaning your data never leaves your device.
    When you paste your JSON code into our website, all processing happens locally in your browser. This ensures that your sensitive information remains under your control at all times.

    No Database Storage: We don't store any of your data in a database. Once you close the app or refresh the page, your JSON code is gone. This eliminates any risk of data exposure or unauthorized access to your information.

    Transparent Operations: We believe in transparency. Our app doesn't perform any hidden operations or transmit your data to external servers. You can trust that what you input into our app stays within your browser and doesn't go anywhere else. Your security is our top priority. If you have any further questions or concerns about the safety of our app, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

  • Why shouldn't I use third-party apps to find unfollowers on Instagram? Using third-party apps for this purpose can pose risks:

    Security: They may require your login credentials, risking account security.
    Terms of Service: It might violate Instagram's terms, leading to account suspension.
    Accuracy: Results may be unreliable due to API limitations.
    Privacy: Some apps collect and share personal data, compromising privacy.
    For safety, it's best to use Instagram's built-in features or trusted tools that prioritize security and privacy.
